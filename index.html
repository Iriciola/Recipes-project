Mulligatawny Soup

Mulligatawny is a soup which originated from South Indian cuisine. The name originates from the Tamil words miḷagu, and taṇṇi; literally, "pepper-water". It is related to the dish rasam. Main ingredients commonly include chicken, mutton, and lentils
